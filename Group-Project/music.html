About Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish is a Grammy-winning artist known for her haunting vocals and genre-defying music. Her breakout single "Ocean Eyes" brought her to international stardom, and she continues to push boundaries in both music and fashion.

When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? (2019)

Happier Than Ever (2021)

About Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus is an American singer, songwriter, and actress, known for her distinctive raspy voice and ability to master various music genres. Starting her career as a teen idol on Disney Channel's Hannah Montana, Miley evolved into a bold and versatile artist, releasing chart-topping albums like Bangerz and Plastic Hearts. Her music and persona have continued to push boundaries, solidifying her as a pop culture icon.

Bangerz (2013)

Plastic Hearts (2020)

About Rihanna

Rihanna is a global music icon, business mogul, and fashion influencer. Known for her distinctive voice and groundbreaking hits, Rihanna has released eight studio albums, sold millions of records worldwide, and won multiple Grammy Awards. Beyond her music career, she has launched successful ventures such as Fenty Beauty and Savage X Fenty, becoming a trailblazer in the beauty and fashion industries.

Anti (2016)

Loud (2010)

Good Girl Gone Bad (2007)
