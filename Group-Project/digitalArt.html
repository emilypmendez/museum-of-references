Digital Art

IDigitral Art is a medium based around creating content using a computer. This involves moving images, photoshop, 3D moddeling, and compositing on the web. everything is printed and created using pixels or vectors. There are many digital artists right now and a great place to see the growth of this medium is the "Museum Of Moving Images" located in Queens New York.

Growth of Digital Art

  • Social Commentary
  • Community Engagement
  • Controversy and Division
  • Potential for Misinformation
  • Artistic Recognition
  • Inviting to other mediums
