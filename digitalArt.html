Digital Art

Digitral Art is a medium based around creating content using a computer. This involves moving images, photoshop, 3D moddeling, and compositing on the web. everything is printed and created using pixels or vectors. There are many digital artists right now and a great place to see the growth of this medium is the "Museum Of Moving Images" located in Queens New York.
