90s Paintings

In the 90s Graffiti was popular, as street art gained importance. The 90s was a period of experimentation in the art world and help to set the stage for a lot of the contemporary practices today.

Impact of Grafitti on Communities

  • Social Commentary
  • Community Engagement
  • Controversy and Division
  • Potential for Misinformation
  • Artistic Recognition
  • Tourism Attraction
  • Resistance to Gentrification
  • Public Discourse
